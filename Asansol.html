ASANSOL COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

The Asansol Engineering College or AEC is a self-financing college in West Bengal, India offering undergraduate and postgraduate courses in Engineering and Technology and other allied fields. It was established in 1998 by The Academy of Engineers as a joint venture between the JIS group and the Techno India group. CONTACT US
