Tagore Engineering college

Rabindranath Tagore University, formerly AISECT University, is a private university established by All India Society for Electronics & Computer Technology in Mendua Village in Raisen District, Madhya Pradesh, India[1][2] and has a Branch in Bhopal. Rabindranath Tagore University is recognised by University Grant Commission and the Madhya Pradesh State Government. It provides academic programmes in graduate, post graduate and research levels in Engineering, Information Technology, Management, Law, Science, Arts, Commerce, Education, Paramedical etc. AISECT Academy for Fire, Safety & Security was launched with the support of NAFS– National Academy of Fire & Safety Engineering CONTACT US
